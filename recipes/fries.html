Air Fryer French Fries

  1. 1 pound russet potatoes, peeled
  2. 2 teaspoons vegetable oil
  3. 1 pinch cayenne pepper
  4. Â½ teaspoon kosher salt

Directions

  1. Step 1

    2. Cut each potato lengthwise into 3/8-inch-thick slices, then slice sections into 3/8-inch-wide sticks.

  2. Step 2

    3. Place potatoes in a bowl of cold water. Let soak to release excess starches, about 5 minutes; drain.

  3. Step 3

    4. Pour boiling water over the potatoes until they're covered by a few inches. Let sit for 10 minutes.

  4. Step 4

    5. Drain potatoes and transfer onto some paper towels. Blot off excess water and let cool completely, at least 10 minutes.

  5. Step 5

    6. Place cooled potatoes in a mixing bowl. Drizzle with oil and season with cayenne; toss to coat.

  6. Step 6

    7. Preheat an air fryer to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).

  7. Step 7

    8. Stack potatoes in a double layer in the fryer basket. Cook for 15 minutes. Slide basket out and toss fries. Continue frying until golden brown, about 10 minutes more.

  8. Step 8

    9. Toss fries with salt in a mixing bowl. Serve immediately.
